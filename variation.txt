# Neverness to Everness (NTE) - A/B Copywriting Playbook & CTA Variants
Project: Official PC Lander

Below are 3 high-converting copywriting + CTA variants tailored to the target Windows PC gamer persona (16-35, high energy, skeptical of generic F2P mobile-slop).

==========================================================================
VARIANT 1: THE ANTI-SLOP CHALLENGER (PAS - Problem-Agitate-Solve)
==========================================================================
Target Persona Focus: Skeptics tired of generic pay-to-win, lazy mobile ports.
First-Word Prominence: YES ("FREE")

[Main Hook Headline]
FREE PLAY: Tired of Pay-To-Win Slop? Try the Ultimate Anime GTA on PC!

[Pulsing Urgency Badge]
🔥 LIMIT-TIME LAUNCH OFFER • ⭐ 4.9/5 RATED • 8.5M+ NATIVE INSTALLS

[Popup / Main CTA Button Text]
PLAY FREE NOW

[Sub-CTA Text]
Windows PC Launcher (100% Free)

[Trust Microcopy]
No credit card • No sign-up • Virus-free

[Psychological Framework & Rationale]
- Framework: PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve)
- Problem: The market is flooded with pay-to-win, low-quality game ports.
- Agitate: Gamers are sick of being treated like wallets for "mobile slop."
- Solve: A native, high-fidelity next-gen urban RPG that is actually premium and 100% free.
- "FREE" is placed as the very first word to capture the #1 conversion trigger.
